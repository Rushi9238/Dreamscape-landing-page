About Yodaplus DocuTrade

At Yodaplus, we're committed to revolutionizing trade finance with our innovative documentation solution, compliant with the Model Law on Electronic Transferable Records (MLETR). Our unique digital solution not only simplifies the complex process of trade documentation but also ensures that digital documents possess the same legal credibility as traditional paper documents. This results in a secure and efficient trade environment, enabling you to focus on what truly matters - your business growth.

Our innovative solution is transforming the trade finance sector by bridging the substantial 'trade finance gap', which has previously limited the potential of many Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs). We believe in the potential of SMEs, and our platform serves as an enabling hub, helping these businesses access the liquidity they need to flourish.

Harnessing the power of the Open Attestation platform for verifiable documents and transferable records, we've developed a ground breaking solution thatexpertly manages the lifecycle of trade documents. This technology allows for the smooth facilitation of finance based on trade documents, aiding SMEs in fulfilling their working capital requirements with the reassurance of digital collateral. Our solution adheres to multiple jurisdiction laws, offering a secure and trustworthy platform for all users.

With our robust and secure digital solution, we are setting a new standard in the trade finance sector. We invite you to experience the future of trade finance with Yodaplus and unlock new growth possibilities for your business today.
